# TreeTangler Demo

## Starting Up

⏳ **Please wait** - The app is building and will open automatically in a new browser tab (usually takes 30-60 seconds).

---

## Using TreeTangler

### Load Example Trees
1. Click **"Load Left Tree"** → select `trees/Orthobunyavirus_L`
2. Click **"Load Right Tree"** → select `trees/Orthobunyavirus_M`
3. Trees appear side-by-side with connecting lines

### Detangle Trees
- Click **"Detangle Left"** or **"Detangle Right"** to minimize crossing lines
- Click nodes to swap children and manually adjust tree layout
- Use zoom/pan controls to explore

### Try Other Examples
- `trees/Nairovirus_L` and `trees/Nairovirus_M`
- `trees/Phlebovirus_L` and `trees/Phlebovirus_M`

---

## Closing

1. **Close the app tab** in your browser
2. **Stop Codespace**: 
   - Click your profile (bottom left)
   - Select "Stop Current Codespace"
   - Or let it auto-stop after 30 minutes of inactivity

**Codespaces are free for 60 hours/month** - remember to stop when done!
